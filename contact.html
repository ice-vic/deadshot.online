Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will you respond to my message?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, we prioritize responses and aim to get back to you sooner.

Can I report another player?

Yes, if you encounter inappropriate behavior or cheating, please report it to us. Include player username, time, and description of the incident when possible.

Do you accept game suggestions?

Absolutely! We love hearing ideas from our community. Share your suggestions for new features, game modes, or improvements by emailing us at our contact address.
